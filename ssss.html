THE CHALLENGE: The Reality of Waste in India

Waste management is a major challenge in India, leading to severe environmental and health issues.

  • Environmental Impact: Poor waste management leads to soil, air, and water pollution.
  • Health Issues: This directly or indirectly causes health problems like asthma, cancer, cholera, typhoid, and hepatitis B/C.
  • Societal Impact: The lack of proper waste disposal results in unpleasant odors, poor quality of life, and unhygienic surroundings.
  • Systemic Failure: The mismatch in garbage collection truck timings often leads to waste piles accumulating in public spaces.
